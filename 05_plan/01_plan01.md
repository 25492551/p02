# Riemann Zeta Function Visualization and Simulation Plan (Plan 01)

## Project Background

The P01 project conducted research on prime distribution and the Riemann hypothesis. The P02 project explores the mathematical properties and physical interpretations of non-trivial zeros of the Riemann zeta function through visualization and simulation.

## Script Analysis Results

### Current Scripts in 03_script Folder

1. **01_non_commutative_noise_simulation.py - Riemann Zeta Function Simulation (Non-commutative Noise)**
   - Approximate calculation of the Riemann zeta function
   - Application of non-commutative noise
   - Comparative visualization of Order and Chaos

2. **02_energy_landscape_visualization.py - Energy Landscape Visualization**
   - Energy landscape calculation on the complex plane
   - Sum of order energy and chaos energy
   - Stability analysis of zeros

3. **03_particle_simulation.py - Particle Simulation**
   - Particles moving along the gradient of the energy function
   - Visualization of trajectories converging near zeros
   - Application of stochastic noise (quantum fluctuations)

4. **04_vector_field_visualization.py - Vector Field Visualization**
   - Vector field calculation based on energy gradients
   - Flow where zeros act as 'sinks'
   - Streamline visualization

5. **05_coulomb_gas_simulation.py - Coulomb Gas Simulation**
   - Simulation of repulsion between zeros
   - Explanation of why multiple roots are impossible
   - Pair correlation analysis

6. **07_zero_prediction.py - Zero Prediction**
   - Macroscopic prediction: Using Riemann-von Mangoldt formula
   - Microscopic prediction: Using Coulomb repulsion and GUE properties
   - Comparison of statistical and physical methods

7. **08_zeta_bell_sound_synthesis.py - Zeta Bell Sound Generation**
   - Conversion of Riemann zeta function zeros to frequencies
   - Generation of non-commutative harmonies
   - Sound file (.wav) generation

8. **09_spectral_rigidity_prediction.py - Spectral Rigidity Prediction**
   - Zero prediction using spectral rigidity
   - Utilization of inverse function of Riemann-von Mangoldt formula
   - Deviation correction based on GUE theory

9. **10_chaos_wave_prediction.py - Chaos Wave Prediction**
   - Precise zero prediction using Riemann-Siegel Z-function approximation
   - Calculation of wave interference of primes
   - Root finding using chaos wave function
